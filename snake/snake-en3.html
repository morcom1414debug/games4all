Audio Snakes and Ladders: Dimension Chaos 🎲

  • Objective: An audio-based snakes and ladders game designed for visually impaired players. Take turns rolling the dice to reach space 100.
  • Card System: Hold up to 2 cards in your hand to disrupt the bot or protect yourself.
  • Obstacles: Watch out for snakeheads, banana peels, and quicksand (which traps you and forces you to lose 1 turn).
  • Box Duel Minigame: When both tokens land on the exact same space, players draw a random mystery box to settle the score.
